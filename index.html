About

Here at DrivEz, we believe that private driving learners deserve better than shady-looking and self-promoting instructor websites. We believe that our private driving instructors deserve a reliable and centralised platform for students to place their honest reviews. We believe that the private driving experience can be so much more when the right information is just clicks away.

FAQs

After you register with us, there will be instructors to search and filter around. Once you are satisfied with the instructor, click on the instructor's information and look for the instructor's contacts under the "About" tab.

The instructor's ratings and reviews are only shown after you have registered with us. This is to curb trolls and help to better ensure that the ratings and reviews are genuine.

After you have registered with us, you will be able to leave a rating and review for the instructor. Simply click on the instructor's information and look for the "Leave a review" button.

Simply click on the "Register" button above the "Above" section and fill in the required information.

You can have your own profile page on DrivEz, which will allow you to showcase your achievements and qualifications. You will also be able to receive ratings and reviews from your students, which will help you to improve your teaching methods and gain more students.
